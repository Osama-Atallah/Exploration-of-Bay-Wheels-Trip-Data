by Osama Atallah¶

Investigation Overview¶

For this investigation I used "Ford GoBike System Data", I looked at bike hiring times and stations longitudes and latitudes to reveal their effect on the station hiring activity (How many bikes it hires).

Dataset Overview¶

This data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area for year 2019. The data has information of 2,506,983 rides and 334 stations. This information includes: start date and time, start station, end station, ride duration, longitudes and latitudes of start and end stations. Start and dnd stations are one set of stations. 433,555 data points (and 20 stations) were removed from the investigation due to inconsistencies or missing information.

Distribution of Station Hiring Activity¶

The distribution of station hiring activity covers large scale of values. Plotted on a logarithmic scale, the distribution takes on a multimodal shape. The highest peaks are between ~ 2000 and 10000.

Station categories¶

I splitted stations into four categories based on their hiring activity quartiles:

Category A: Stations that hired less than 2031 bikes.
Category B: Stations that hired more than 2031 bikes and less than 4543 bikes.
Category C: Stations that hired more than 4543 bikes and less than 8859 bikes.
Category D: Stations that hired more than 8859 bikes.

Station Category by Day of Week and Hiring Activity¶

The busier the stations are, the larger the difference between hired bikes count in weekdays and in weekends.

Station Location by Station Category¶

  • The stations are geo-seperated into two groups, East Bay and West Bay. East Bay is at the North East and West Bay is at the South West and they are seperated by water.
  • West bay has larger number of stations than the East bay.
  • Most of the busiest stations (categories D and C) are in West Bay area while East Bay has mostly less busier stations (categories A and B).
  • In the West Bay area, Category D stations are concentrated at North East (the busiest region of the Bay) by and near the see.
  • As you move inside the land towards South West, category C stations dominate.
  • Category B are scaterred here and there withen these two regions while category A stations mostly reside outside the busy regions.
